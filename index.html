Protect the bees, save the environment! Experience an exciting journey as a bee navigating through challenges, collecting flowers, and supporting local farmers. This game combines fun gameplay with environmental awareness, encouraging players to make a real-world impact.

Bees transfer pollen, helping plants reproduce and contributing to about 75% of global food production.

Bees are declining due to pesticides, habitat loss, and climate change.

The loss of bees reduces crop yields and disrupts ecosystems, threatening our food supply and environmental balance.

Problem Statement

The global bee population is experiencing a dramatic decline due to factors such as habitat loss, pesticide use, climate change, and diseases. This issue affects not only farmers and industries that rely on agriculture but also the entire ecosystem. Bees are key pollinators for plants, and their decline can lead to reduced crop production and threats to biodiversity. Particularly, a significant portion of the food consumed by humans is pollinated by bees, so their extinction poses serious risks to food security.

Reason behind the machanics

I wanted to make fun the top priority so that people can enjoy the game for a long time while also adding information about bee extinction throughout the gameplay to help raise awareness.

I also decided to create a short and simple game that busy students and working professionals can play quickly, rather than a long game that takes up a lot of time. Not only focusing on immersing players in the game itself, I emphasized making it easy to share with others so that many people can play it.

project objective

You play as a worker bee. Your goal is to find flowers, collect nectar and pollen, and help your colony survive. But outside the hive, there are many dangers trying to stop you. Use helpful items and try to gather as many flowers as you can!.

Collect Flowers, Learn About Bees, and Feel the Competition! Share the Game!

The honey dipper represents the bee's main food, honey. In the game, it serves as a health recovery item, highlighting the importance of honey for the bee's survival and well-being.

Pollen isn’t a weapon, but it’s something bees naturally collect and carry. In the game, the pollen floats alongside the bee and can be used to slow down enemies, helping the player escape.

The flower pot lets players plant obstacles (bushes). It helps the bee hide from enemies and create more flowers in real life, which are essential for the bee’s food.

The farmer figure symbolizes supporting local farmers and eco-friendly practices, like avoiding harmful pesticides and reducing carbon emissions, which help protect bees and fight climate change. When players purchase this item, it unlocks the bee’s dash ability.

Some people might think that taking honey from the bees is harmful, but in reality, honey production helps the beekeeping industry, which benefits the environment and the bees. This gives the Max HP higher for the player.

Box of seeds, in which the flower appears 2X more during gameplay. This is really helpful for collecting coins and points but also ties into the environmental aspect of the game. Planting flowers helps fight deforestation, which is helpful to bees in the real world.
